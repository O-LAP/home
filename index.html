Computational Furniture

Writing code that does furniture design.

...and no...this is not smart furniture.

Good design is a lot about numbers
and hey computers are really good at that
So when we wrote code to design furniture
It blew our minds!

Because we could do stuff like...

Personalize your cosy chair.

The code understand your body parameters and adapts the design...just...for...YOU!

...which is sent to a CAM(Computer Aided Manufacturing, similar to 3D printing) fabricator near you.

High precision CAM tools ensure the standard quality.

Enabling a decentralized and localized production benefitting the communities.

Every design is assesed only for how good the design is. The suppliers and distributors do not get a chance to influence what you see.
